Your Trusted Pharmaceutical Export Partner

Built on regulatory expertise and trusted manufacturing partnerships

V3 Life Care Exim is a pharmaceutical merchant exporter engaged in sourcing and supplying a wide range of pharmaceutical products through a verified network of qualified manufacturing partners. As a pharmaceutical sourcing and export company, we are committed to delivering quality pharmaceutical products backed by in-house regulatory expertise and a manufacturing network built through direct professional relationships.

We believe in building long-term business relationships by treating every customer with honesty, professionalism, and respect. With a customer-focused approach and strong regulatory expertise, we strive to make pharmaceutical sourcing simple, transparent, and reliable for every buyer.

Based in Mumbai, India, we make available Tablets, Capsules, Dry Syrups, Pharmaceutical Syrups, Injectables, Ointments, Eye Drops, Nasal Drops, Herbal Products, Nutraceuticals, APIs, Dermal Patches, Transdermal Patches, and many more, all supported by complete regulatory documentation and professional export coordination.
